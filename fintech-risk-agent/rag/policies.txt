[Credit Score Policies]
- Loans above 40000 require manual review if credit score < 650.
- Reject automatically if credit_score < 550.
- Prefer credit_score > 720 for instant approval.

[Income & Employment]
- High risk if employment_years < 2 unless income > 80000.
- Self-employed applicants (employment_years = 0) require 2 years tax returns.

[Debt-to-Income (DTI)]
- DTI > 0.50 is considered critical risk; requires strong compensating factors (e.g., LTV < 60%).
- DTI > 0.43 (Qualified Mortgage limit) triggers enhanced review.
- Conservative limit: DTI should not exceed 0.36 for preferred rates.

[Loan Terms]
- Loan terms > 48 months for amounts < 10000 are not recommended.
- Loans for debt consolidation have higher default rates; capped at 0.40 DTI.
